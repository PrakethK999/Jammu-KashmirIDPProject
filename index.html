Jammu and Kashmir


Jammu and Kashmir, known as the "Paradise on Earth," is a region with diverse landscapes, Himalayan mountains, serene valleys, and a blend of Hindu, Islamic, and Buddhist heritages. Its picturesque landscapes, historic cities like Srinagar and Jammu, and diverse cuisine reflect the region's cultural diversity.
